Fishing

Where I Fish

I mainly fish the backwaters of the mighty mississippi but sometimes I'll fish the local lakes of Winona MN. Every year my fishing buddy and I take an annual fishing.

What I Fish For

I will fish for just about anything but my #1 favorite fish to catch is large mouth bass.

My Favorite Lure

Without a doubt my favorite fishing lures are tope water frogs or anything topwater. I love the adrenaline rush of a fish blowing up out of the water.

Gardening

How I Got Started

I started my gardening journey in my first few years in college. I started by helping a master gardener maintain her garden. I gained alot of knowledge from her and the love of gardening evolved.

Surviving College

Thanks to my love of gardening, I was able to use this hobby to pay rent. I solo landscaped for two years before getting a job in WSU's computer lab.

My Plumeria Collection

I found my first hawaiian plumeria when visiting the MN State Fair. Since then I've propagated many and now have over ten! FYI they grow the flowers that make hawaiian leis.

Fantasy Footbal

My Types of Leagues

I loooooveeee fantasy football. I play in three leagues every year. Two of them are your typically one year league with basic 1/2 ppr scoring. One of which has heavy bonus points. The third and favorite league is a dynasty league where you keep players from one year to the next.

Dynasty League

Like I had mentioned, the dynasty league is my favorite. I love the intense strategy involved in creating and developing your team one piece at a time.

The Keys to Success

The key to winning in dynasty is studying the upcoming rookie class, becoming knowledgable of how to trade and draft properly, and avoiding the injury bug (which is a bit of a dice roll)

Encaustic Art

What Is It?

Encaustic painting is almost the same as your run of the mill painting except you use wax as your medium. It can be challenging because you have to keep the wax warm in order to apply it to your canvas.

My Work

I have created three pieces of encaustic art. My very first piece was wax on cancas and my other two were wax on tile.
